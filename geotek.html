About Geotek Corp

At Geotek Corp, we leverage over 30 years of recruitment expertise and 25 years in IT to connect talented individuals with innovative companies. Our approach is built on integrity, deep industry insights, and a commitment to lasting partnerships.

Founded by George Nikitiadis, Geotek Corp has grown to become a trusted partner for organizations seeking top-tier talent and individuals looking for their next career opportunity. Our commitment to excellence and personal connection sets us apart in the industry.

We pride ourselves on our deep understanding of the technical landscape and our ability to identify not just skill matches, but cultural fits that lead to long-term success for both employers and professionals.

Geotek Corp Website Redesign Rationale

Strategic Vision

The Geotek Corp website redesign is driven by three key principles:

  • Brand Elevation: Position Geotek Corp as a modern, trusted leader in recruitment, consulting, and outsourcing.
  • Dual-Audience Approach: Engage both corporate partners and job seekers with clear, tailored messaging.
  • Modern User Experience: Implement a clean, responsive design that emphasizes professionalism while providing intuitive navigation.

Key Design Improvements

Previous Website

  • Static, dated visual presentation
  • Limited mobile responsiveness
  • Generic messaging that doesn't highlight expertise
  • Limited user engagement features
  • Unclear pathways for different user types

New Website

  • Dynamic, modern visual design with animations
  • Fully responsive across all devices
  • Tailored messaging highlighting 30+ years expertise
  • Interactive elements that encourage engagement
  • Clear pathways for both clients and job seekers

Technical Enhancements

The redesign incorporates several technical improvements:

  • Performance Optimization: Faster load times through optimized code and modern web technologies.
  • Accessibility: WCAG-compliant design ensuring the site is usable by all visitors.
  • SEO Enhancement: Structured content and semantic HTML to improve search engine visibility.
  • Analytics Integration: Enhanced tracking capabilities to measure user engagement and conversion.

Content Strategy

The content has been restructured to:

  • Emphasize George Nikitiadis's extensive experience (30+ years recruiting, 25+ years in IT)
  • Showcase client testimonials prominently to build credibility
  • Clearly communicate the three core service offerings (Recruitment, Consulting, Outsourcing)
  • Create clear calls-to-action for both clients and candidates

This strategic content approach ensures visitors quickly understand Geotek Corp's value proposition and expertise, encouraging deeper engagement.

Expected Impact

The redesigned website is expected to deliver significant improvements in:

  • Increased conversion rates for both client inquiries and candidate applications
  • Extended time on site through engaging, valuable content
  • Higher search engine rankings for relevant industry terms
  • Improved brand perception as a modern, technology-forward recruitment partner
  • More qualified leads through clear audience targeting

Mobile Experience Focus

We've prioritized mobile optimization throughout the site, with special attention to:

  • Adaptive Content: Layouts that automatically adjust based on screen size and orientation
  • Touch-Optimized: Larger tap targets for buttons and navigation elements
  • Performance: Optimized graphics and code for fast loading on mobile networks
  • Device-Specific: Special handling for notched devices and varying screen dimensions
  • Simplified Navigation: Streamlined menu system for smaller screens
