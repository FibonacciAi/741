Create Your Zora Collection

  • 1
    Connect to Zora

    Visit zora.co/create and connect your wallet. Make sure your wallet is on the Base network for lower fees and faster transactions.

    You can use Coinbase Wallet, Rainbow, or MetaMask—all support Base network.

  • 2
    Create Biscuit Collection

    After connecting, select Create → New Collection. Upload your best photos of Biscuit.

    Add a heartfelt description about Biscuit and your fundraising goals. Include links to your GoFundMe in the description.

    Add the tag #BiscuitMemorial to improve discoverability.

  • 3
    Configure NFT Settings

    You have two pricing options:

    • Fixed Price: Set an affordable price like 0.005 ETH (~$15) to maximize participation

    • Free Mint: Allow free claiming with an optional donation feature

    Set Creator Earnings to 10% to receive royalties from secondary sales—direct these to your fundraiser.

  • 4
    Promote & Engage

    Share your collection link across platforms:

    • Add the Zora collection link to your GoFundMe page

    • Share regular updates on social media with #BiscuitOnChain

    • Create short videos showing Biscuit's journey and how funds are helping

    Post updates directly on Zora using their "Posts" feature—connecting with your NFT holders creates a lasting community.

Base Network Advantage

Creating your collection on Base through Zora gives you significantly lower gas fees than Ethereum Mainnet, making it more accessible for supporters to participate in honoring Biscuit.
