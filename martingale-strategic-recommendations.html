Martingale Funnel Fix

Strategic framework to scale from 2 to 20-30 orders per day

Comprehensive Growth Strategy

A holistic approach to optimizing the entire customer journey

The Martingale Funnel Fix provides a structured framework to systematically increase online sales volume by addressing each stage of the customer journey. This approach focuses on both optimizing existing touchpoints and implementing new strategies to drive growth.

1. Awareness

  • Leverage star power

    Monthly IG Lives with Donnie & D-Nice + CTA to buy.

  • Own "cocktail culture"

    Reels + blog on gifting, food pairings, cocktails.

  • Paid boost

    Meta + Google Ads with lookalikes and intent keywords.

  • Performance Max

    Google Performance Max campaigns to reach new intent-based audiences efficiently.

  • City Hive strategy

    Syndicate content across retailer platforms for visibility and backlinks.

2. Consideration

  • Klaviyo welcome flow

    4-part email series tied to cocktail booklet.

  • Add social proof

    Press, awards, and UGC on product pages. Highlight quotes from Forbes, Esquire, etc.

  • Repurpose media mentions

    Editorialize features for SEO instead of reposting verbatim.

  • Thought-leadership content

    Target whiskey drinkers new to cognac, seasonal use cases, and gifting guides.

  • Interactive moments

    Virtual tastings, livestream cocktail classes, co-hosted with retail/E-comm partners.

3. Conversion

  • Express checkout

    Add Apple Pay, Shop Pay, Klarna, Affirm.

  • Bundles = AOV boost

    Cocktail kits, gifting sets, seasonal offerings.

  • Exit intent

    Offer 10% or free shipping nudge based on cart size.

  • Transparent pricing

    Show shipping cost & ETA on product and cart pages (e.g. "Ships in 3--5 days").

  • Urgency cues

    Low inventory alerts, limited edition copy, restock notifications.

  • Upgrade nudges

    "Add a 375ml for 30% off," "Spend $20 more for free shipping."

4. Retention

  • Connoisseurs Club

    Points for buys, referrals, UGC -- tiered rewards, early access.

  • Post-purchase flow

    Thank you + reorder + refer-a-friend + cocktail content.

  • Unboxing moment

    QR to playlist, recipes, artist collab print/postcard.

  • Review collection

    Automate post-purchase review asks. Integrate with Trustpilot, Yotpo, or Mailchimp flows.

  • Re-engage email flows

    For lapsed buyers and high-value seasonal nudges.

Bonus Big Bets

Crypto checkout

Offer crypto payment with low-lift plugin (via Nexus or 3rd-party).

Web3 loyalty experiment

Token-gated access to content, merch, or events.

Golden ticket campaign

Win a trip to Cognac, plant a grapevine, or intern at the castle.

Micro-surprise gifting

Postcard pack, bottle sweater, or socks with each 2-bottle+ order.

"How do we grow without more budget? By squeezing every ounce of value from existing traffic, automating smartly, and activating our brand community."
